About Us

Founding History

The Tanzania Women Hope Organization was founded by a group of women who faced various challenges—health, financial, psychological, and cultural. They came together to create a proper way to make their voices heard, grow, and uplift other women facing similar challenges along with their children.

Our Purpose and Goals

  • To provide women with education and skills
  • To maintain a safe and caring environment
  • To improve living standards
  • To fight against HIV/AIDS and sexually transmitted diseases
  • To initiate economic projects
  • To engage the community

Organizational Structure

General Assembly

Composed of all members and responsible for approving the constitution and electing leaders.

Executive Committee

Leads the organization’s daily operations and implements the decisions made by the general assembly.

Project Committees

Special committees formed to implement specific projects and dissolved after project completion.

Types of Membership

Founding Members

Members who established the organization and applied for its registration. They are the main signatories of the organization’s documents.

Ordinary Members

Members who joined after the organization’s formation and abide by the organization’s constitution.

Approved Members

These members are granted the right to participate in meetings, vote, be elected, and fully engage in all organization activities.

Honorary Members

Individuals or organizations that have contributed to the development of the organization in one way or another.
